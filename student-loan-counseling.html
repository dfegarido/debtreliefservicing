Student Loan Counseling

Expert guidance to help you understand and manage your student loan debt.

What is student loan counseling?

Student loan counseling from Debt Relief Servicing helps you understand your student loan options, repayment plans, and strategies for managing your student debt. Our certified counselors work with you to review your loans, explore repayment options, and create a personalized plan to help you successfully manage your student loan debt.

Whether you're just starting to repay your loans, struggling with payments, or exploring options like loan consolidation or forgiveness, our counselors can provide the guidance and support you need.

What can student loan counseling do for you?

  • Understand your loans - Learn about your loan types, balances, interest rates, and repayment terms
  • Explore repayment options - Discover income-driven repayment plans, deferment, and forbearance options
  • Understand loan consolidation - Learn about federal loan consolidation and whether it's right for you
  • Explore loan forgiveness - Understand eligibility for Public Service Loan Forgiveness and other forgiveness programs
  • Create a repayment strategy - Develop a plan to manage your student loans while meeting your other financial goals

How does student loan counseling work?

Our student loan counseling sessions are designed to help you understand and manage your student loan debt. During your session, we'll review your loan information, discuss your financial situation, and explore options for repayment, consolidation, and forgiveness.

Our certified counselors will help you understand all aspects of student loans, from repayment plans to loan forgiveness programs. We'll also provide you with resources and tools to help you stay on track with your student loan payments.
