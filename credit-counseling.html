Credit Counseling Services

Simple, judgment-free solutions to debt, credit, and budget problems.

What is credit counseling?

Credit counseling is a service designed to help consumers find solutions to common financial challenges.

Alternatively known as consumer credit counseling, debt counseling, and debt & budget counseling, credit counseling can help you learn how to manage overwhelming debt, poor credit, budget deficits and more. These counseling conversations are always kept confidential and focus on meeting you where you are, developing a plan to meet your financial goals and achieve credit card debt relief.

Debt Relief Servicing is a nonprofit credit counseling and financial education company with NFCC-certified counselors. We take the time to thoroughly review your budget, discuss your concerns, and provide specific advice on how to reach your unique goals. Best of all, we offer credit counseling for free, 24/7.

What can credit counseling do for me?

Peace of mind and no more stressful calls from creditors. Our credit counseling services are all about breaking free from the cycle of crushing debt and ever-increasing expenses. We understand that complicated problems require personalized solutions, which is why our counselors work so hard to make sure your needs and concerns are heard and understood.

As part of this confidential conversation, we'll discuss ways to help you save money, find relief from credit debt, and start working toward your top financial goals. You'll leave your free session with an action plan, access to valuable resources, and — if it's right for you — the opportunity to save money and accelerate your debt repayment with a debt management plan.

Credit counseling from Debt Relief Servicing is an excellent, obligation-free way to start finding real, lasting credit card debt relief. Start your free credit counseling session today.

How does credit counseling work?

Credit counseling is a great way to step back and get clarity on your financial situation. During your session, we'll review your income, expenses, and debts in detail to create a complete picture of where your money is going. It's helpful to have your most recent bills and statements on hand so that we can get a better understanding of what your current situation looks like.

Next, we discuss your current goals and long-term financial plans, providing unbiased suggestions and resources designed to help you reach those goals. Through our credit counseling services, we'll provide the support and practical advice you need to make lifelong changes to your financial outlook and get out of debt.

The initial counseling session usually takes less than an hour to complete over the phone, or as little as 15 minutes online. During this session, we can help you create a budget and share options for eliminating debt. You can complete your free credit counseling session online, over the phone, or even in-person in select locations.

Credit counseling FAQs

Questions about the credit counseling process? We've got answers.

Does credit counseling cost anything?

No. We offer free credit counseling online and over the phone, 24/7. You may be offered additional fee-based services following your free session, but there is no obligation to use any additional services.

What does a credit counselor do?

With your permission, a credit counselor will pull your credit report and review all your debts, expenses, and sources of income. After thoroughly reviewing your information, they will provide recommendations and send you a customized action plan. Learn more about the role of credit counselors.

What do I need to bring to my credit counseling session?

The more information you have on hand, the better. Gather recent pay stubs, bank statements, creditor statements (loans and credit cards), and utility bills. That said, we can work with whatever information you have available, so don't hesitate to get started. Learn more about preparing for your credit counseling session.

Will you talk to my creditors?

Every Debt Relief Servicing counseling session is completely confidential. We will only contact your creditors if you decide to begin a DMP in order to set up your repayment plan.

How do I start a credit counseling session?

Credit counseling from Debt Relief Servicing is available online or over the phone, 24/7. We also offer in-person sessions at select locations if you call ahead to schedule an appointment.
