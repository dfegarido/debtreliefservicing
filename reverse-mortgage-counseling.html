Reverse Mortgage Counseling

Required HUD counseling to help you understand if a reverse mortgage is right for you.

What is reverse mortgage counseling?

Reverse mortgage counseling is a required step for anyone considering a Home Equity Conversion Mortgage (HECM), the most common type of reverse mortgage. Our HUD-certified counselors provide unbiased, confidential counseling to help you understand how reverse mortgages work, the costs involved, and whether a reverse mortgage is the right financial decision for your situation.

During your counseling session, we'll review your financial situation, discuss your goals, and help you understand all aspects of reverse mortgages, including eligibility requirements, payment options, and potential risks.

What can reverse mortgage counseling do for you?

  • Understand reverse mortgages - Learn how reverse mortgages work and how they differ from traditional mortgages
  • Evaluate your options - Explore whether a reverse mortgage is the right choice for your financial situation
  • Understand costs and fees - Learn about all the costs associated with reverse mortgages
  • Explore payment options - Understand the different ways you can receive funds from a reverse mortgage
  • Understand your obligations - Learn about your responsibilities as a reverse mortgage borrower

How does reverse mortgage counseling work?

Reverse mortgage counseling is required by HUD before you can apply for a reverse mortgage. Our HUD-certified counselors will work with you one-on-one to ensure you fully understand reverse mortgages and can make an informed decision.

During your counseling session, we'll review your financial situation, discuss your goals, and help you understand all aspects of reverse mortgages. Upon completion, you'll receive a certificate that you'll need when applying for a reverse mortgage.
