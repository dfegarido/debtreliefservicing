Homebuyer Counseling

Expert guidance to help you navigate the homebuying process and achieve your dream of homeownership.

What is homebuyer counseling?

Homebuyer counseling from Debt Relief Servicing provides you with the knowledge and tools you need to make informed decisions throughout your homebuying journey. Our HUD-certified counselors work with you one-on-one to understand your financial situation, explore your options, and create a personalized plan to achieve your homeownership goals.

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or looking to purchase your next home, our counselors can help you understand the mortgage process, improve your credit, save for a down payment, and navigate the complexities of homebuying.

What can homebuyer counseling do for you?

  • Understand the homebuying process - Learn about mortgages, down payments, closing costs, and more
  • Improve your credit - Get personalized advice on how to boost your credit score
  • Create a savings plan - Develop a strategy to save for your down payment and closing costs
  • Explore loan options - Understand different types of mortgages and which might be right for you
  • Prepare for homeownership - Learn about the responsibilities and costs of owning a home

How does homebuyer counseling work?

Our homebuyer counseling sessions are designed to be flexible and convenient. You can meet with a counselor in person, over the phone, or online. During your session, we'll review your financial situation, discuss your homebuying goals, and create a personalized action plan.

Our HUD-certified counselors will help you understand all aspects of the homebuying process, from improving your credit to choosing the right mortgage. We'll also provide you with resources and tools to help you stay on track as you work toward homeownership.
