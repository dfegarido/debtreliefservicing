Credit Report Reviews

Concerned about your credit? Our experts can help you unlock the secrets of your credit report and start building strong credit for a lifetime.

Understanding your credit report

A little help can go a long way. We provide the tools and guidance to make it easy. Your credit report and credit score influence your ability to get a loan, buy a car, own a home, rent an apartment, find affordable insurance, land a good job, and so much more.

What is a credit report review?

A confidential, one-on-one credit report review from Debt Relief Servicing is a great way to demystify credit. Working with a certified credit counselor, you'll examine all the information shared on your credit report, section by section. Your counselor will help you understand what it all means, how each item is potentially impacting your credit score, and what you can do to improve it.

During your credit report review, we'll help you identify any errors or inaccuracies that may be hurting your credit score, and we'll provide you with a step-by-step action plan for addressing them. We'll also discuss strategies for building and maintaining strong credit going forward.

What can a credit report review do for you?

  • Understand your credit report - Learn what each section means and how it affects your credit score
  • Identify errors and inaccuracies - Spot mistakes that could be hurting your credit
  • Get a personalized action plan - Receive step-by-step guidance on improving your credit
  • Learn credit-building strategies - Discover ways to build and maintain strong credit
  • Improve your financial future - Take control of your credit and open doors to better opportunities

How does a credit report review work?

Getting started with a credit report review is easy. Simply schedule a free session with one of our certified credit counselors. During your session, we'll pull your credit report (with your permission) and go through it together, explaining each section and answering any questions you may have.

Your counselor will help you understand your credit score, identify areas for improvement, and provide you with actionable steps you can take to build better credit. The entire process is confidential and designed to empower you with the knowledge you need to take control of your financial future.
